Miniature Schnauzer Resources

Trusted places to learn more, a few recommended reads, and a form to send us a question.

Trusted External Resources

These national organizations are reliable starting points for breed information, health, and finding reputable breeders or rescues:

  • American Kennel Club — Miniature Schnauzer (opens in a new tab) — official breed overview and standard.
  • American Miniature Schnauzer Club (opens in a new tab) — the national breed club, with breeder and rescue referrals.
  • VCA Animal Hospitals (opens in a new tab) — vet-reviewed articles on schnauzer health.
  • American Veterinary Medical Association (opens in a new tab) — pet-owner guidance on choosing and caring for a dog.

Recommended Reading

  • Established breed handbooks such as The Miniature Schnauzer from reputable dog publishers.
  • Your local breed club newsletter and training-class handouts.
  • Vet-reviewed care sites like those linked above.

Rescue & Adoption

Many wonderful Miniature Schnauzers are looking for homes through breed-specific rescues. The national club (linked above) keeps a rescue directory, and local shelters often have schnauzers and schnauzer mixes waiting for families.
